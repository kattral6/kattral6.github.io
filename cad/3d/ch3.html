Visualizing Your Model

1. HIDE Command

Regenerates a three-dimension model with hiddwn lines

1.  Open a drawing with 2D objects and display in a 3D view.

2.  Choose  View, Hide.

    or

3.  Type HIDE at the command prompt.

Command: hide

2. Visual Styles

A visual style is a collecction of settings that control the display of edges and shadingin the viewport.

1.  Open a drawing with 3D object and display in a 3D view.

2.  Choose View, Visual Styles and one of the following style options.

 

3D Hidden

 

2D Wireframe

 

3D Wireframe

 

Realistic

 

Conceptual

 

3. Visual Style Manager

The Visual Styles Manager display sample images of the visual styles available in the drawing. The

selected visual style is indicated by a yellow border, and its settings are displayed in the panel below

the sample images.

1.   Choose View, Visual Styles Manager...

  or

2.   Type VISUALSTYLES at the command prompt. Command: visualstyles

3.  Choose the desired option from one of those available in the drawing for 2D Wireframe, 3D

Wireframe, 3D Hidden, Realistic, or Conceptual options.
